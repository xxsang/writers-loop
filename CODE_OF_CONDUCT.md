# Code of Conduct

Use direct, respectful collaboration. Critique ideas, documentation, behavior, and implementation quality; do not attack people.

Maintainers may remove contributions, issues, pull requests, or comments that are abusive, discriminatory, harassing, spammy, or unrelated to improving Writer's Loop.

Assume contributors may be writers, editors, researchers, or developers with different levels of technical experience. Keep feedback specific, actionable, and grounded in the project goals.
